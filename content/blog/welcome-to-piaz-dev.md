# Welcome to piaz.dev

We're excited to introduce **piaz.dev** - a software development team dedicated to building open-source tools and privacy-focused services for everyone.

## Our Mission

At piaz.dev, we believe that privacy and freedom are fundamental rights in the digital age. Our mission is to create tools and services that empower users to take control of their online experience.

## What We Do

We specialize in:

- **Multi-platform Development**: Building applications that work seamlessly across Linux, Mac, Windows, Android, iOS, and more
- **Open Source Projects**: Contributing to the global community with transparent, auditable code
- **Privacy-First Services**: Creating alternatives to mainstream platforms that respect user privacy
- **Web Development**: Crafting modern, responsive websites with exceptional user experience

## Our Projects

We're proud to be part of the open-source community. Some of our notable projects include:

- **LibRedirect**: A browser extension that redirects popular sites to privacy-friendly alternatives
- **LibreTube**: An open-source YouTube client for Android with no ads or tracking

## Get in Touch

Whether you need help with a project, want to collaborate, or just have questions about our work, we'd love to hear from you. Reach out to us at [hi@piaz.dev](mailto:hi@piaz.dev) or connect with us on [GitHub](https://github.com/piaz-dev) and [Telegram](https://t.me/piazdev).

Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to building a more open and secure internet together!
